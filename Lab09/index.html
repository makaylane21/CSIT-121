Ham and Pickle Wrap

Made by yours truly, you will never have anything better

The ingredients

  • 1 Soft flour Tortillas
  • 2 Slices of honey ham
  • 1 Tbsp Mayo
  • 1 Dill Pickle Spear

Directions

  1. Lay tortilla on a plate
  2. Take a butter knife and spread 1 Tbsp of mayo on the tortilla
  3. Lay pieces of ham down the middle of the tortilla
  4. Place pickle on top of ham
  5. Roll into a wrap

This is my favorite recipe ever because you can never go wrong with lunch meat and a pickle. The satistfying crunch you hear when you bite into the wrap is like no other. The blend of sweet honey ham and an almost tangy dill pickle gives you the best of both worlds.
